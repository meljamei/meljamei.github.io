Clover

Clover is a web application that helps families find their matches. Families of different ages with different interests and situations can find each other and send friend requests and messages.

Pro Photo

Pro photo is similar to Instagram but with a different purpose. Users can upload their photos into different categories. Other users can then ‘like’ the photo or share it in different accounts, such as Facebook or Twitter.

Technologies: HTML5,CSS, jQuery, Bootstrap, Ruby on Rails, PostgreSQL, Cloudinary and Heroku
